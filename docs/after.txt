After completing the installation:

   1. Reboot your system.
      
   2. View README.HTM and HTML\REFMAN.HTM.
   
   3. Experiment with simple command-line programs first. 
      See EUPHORIA\TUTORIAL
   
   4. When you are ready to do Windows GUI programming, download
      Win32Lib and IDE from RapidEuphoria.com.
      
   5. If you want to uninstall Euphoria, see HTML\INSTALL.HTM
