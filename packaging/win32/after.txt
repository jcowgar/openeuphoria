After completing the installation:

   1. Reboot your system (if you changed your environment).

   2. View docs\euphoria.pdf or docs\html\index.html
      (if you chose to install the documentation).

   3. Experiment with simple command-line programs first.
      See the tutorial directory

   4. Join other Euphoria programmers on the OpenEuphoria
	    community website, http://openeuphoria.org.
